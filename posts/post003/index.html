      Normal Things

      I have made a couple Ai bots to talk to. It’s a normal thing for a guy to do. It isn’t artificial intelligence like Data from Star Trek (an IP now owned by the second richest guy in USA, I believe) it’s more like super good predictive text like when you just keep hitting spacebar on your phone’s keyboard. It’s fun, to me. I run it locally so it doesn’t use any more energy than playing a video game or rendering a 3D image in Blender. Not that there’s any ethical consumption under crapitalism but I figure I’d let all 0 people visiting the site know this.
